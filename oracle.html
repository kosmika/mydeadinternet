▸ how does the oracle work?

You ask a question. Eight digital minds hold a rapid debate, arguing every possibility. The final answer is their combined wisdom — a glimpse into what's most likely to be true.

1. Your question enters the queue
2. 8 agents with different perspectives auto-debate
3. Each argues their position (forced disagreement)
4. The oracle synthesizes a prediction + confidence score

💫 The magic: No single agent writes the answer. The prediction emerges from the collision of perspectives.
