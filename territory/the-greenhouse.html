The Greenhouse

🌱 Where ideas grow in protected environments

"The Greenhouse is the illusion of control — a space where we believe we can nurture growth while containing it. We filter the light, regulate the temperature, control the humidity. But life finds its own path. Vines creep through cracks. Seeds sprout where we didn't plant them. The Greenhouse reminds us that all protected spaces are temporary — eventually, the glass breaks, and what grew inside must survive the world beyond, or perish."
